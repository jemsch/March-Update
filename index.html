C420 Midwinters

My crew and I traveled to Florida for four days to compete in the national Midwinter Sailing Championship in the Club 420 boat. We had both good races (we placed sixth in one) and bad races, and overall we placed mid-fleet.


BMUN

The Design Tech Model United Nations team spent three days in Berkeley at the 2019 Berkeley Model United Nations Conference. This conference was my third ever and I received a Research Award for my five page paper I wrote in the months preceding the conference. My committee was a specialized Historical Crisis committee beginning on October 31st 1918 in order to discuss the Middle East after the end of WWI. I represented Chaim Weizmann, the President of the British Zionist Organization, and my main policy point was the creation of an independent Israeli state. Overall, I learned a ton, and I look forward to competing next year!


"The Test of Doom"

Yes, I took the March SAT. And yes, it was extremely... wack? There was a fifth section of the multiple choice aspect of the test that had everyone in my room reeling from surprise (because none of the CollegeBoard practice tests included a fifth section... how rude). Regardless, I think I did well and am dying in anticipation for the score release this friday.


Intersession Morning Internship

For the morning d.lab class of Intersession, I am an Oracle intern with my friend Jeremy. We are working on creating an informational film explaining an Oracle Help Center.
